Recipe #3

  • Time: 20min
  • Serves: Hot
Ingredients Quantity
All-purposes flour 1 1/2 cup
Baking powder 3 1/2 teaspoons
egg 1
milk 1 1/4 cup
salt 1 teaspoon
sugar 1 tablespoon
melted butter 3 table spoons

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
Step2: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
